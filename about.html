Professional Biography

Mayumi Arimitsu, PhD
Juneau, Alaska USA
marimitsu@oceanbight.com

My research interests include documenting the status and trends of rare or threatened species, understanding the scale-dependent influence of glacier dynamics on coastal fjord ecosystems, and the identifying impacts of novel ocean climate patterns on the functioning of marine food webs.
